In a nondescript warehouse on the outskirts of Albuquerque, an ordinary high school chemistry teacher transforms into a notorious drug lord. Surrounded by beakers and burners, he concocts his signature blue methamphetamine, his face obscured by a gas mask to conceal his identity. The air is thick with tension as his partner nervously watches over the process, their fate intertwined with each batch they produce. Outside, the desert sun beats down on the barren landscape, a stark contrast to the illicit activities unfolding within.
